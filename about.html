The Committee

AEROVJTI is an aero-modelling club at the Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute.

It is run by the students themselves- the senior ones teaching,guiding and sharing their knowledge with fellow junior aviation enthusiasts.

After gaining enough experience about the design and construction of radio-controlled aircraft, we aim at going for international competitions like the widely renowned American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics(AIAA)'s Design/Build/Fly held at the U.S.A every year. Apart from AIAA's D/B/F , we have also competed at the Future Flight Design (FFD) competition held in Turkey and stood third internationally in the year 2015.

We conduct workshops and lecture sessions on aero-modelling in various colleges, schools and NGOs.
